Dalgona Coffe Recipe

by Chef Ananya

This yummy South Korean coffe treat that's all over the internet. It requires only four ingredients to make - all which you probably have in the cupborad - you feel like you've won the jackpot, right?

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee or Expresso Coffee
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step: 1 Combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water:
Add two tablespoons of instant coffee, 2 tablespoons of sugar, and 2tsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl

Step: 2 Whisk, Whisk, Whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
Using a electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy and holds stiff peaks when whisk is removed

Step: 3 Serve with milk
Use a spoon to granish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffe cream. Serve with straw and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
